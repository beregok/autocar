About us

Auto Repair is committed to earning your trust by providing the expertise, value and responsiveness you expect.

Full ServiceAuto Repair Center

With over 1400 locations across the U.S. and Canada, Auto Repair technicians can perform preventive maintenance and car repair wherever you are. Every Auto Repair store is staffed by ASE-certified mechanics and we’re proud to have helped form the Motorist Assurance Program (MAP), an independent industry group that establishes best practices for automotive maintenance and auto repair.

Auto Repair offers complete auto care for your vehicle. Whether it’s time for your next factory recommended maintenance visit, a routine oil change, new tires, or repair services on your brakes, muffler and exhaust, suspension, air conditioner, or any other mechanical or electrical component of your car, Auto Repair is in your neighborhood and ready to serve you.

Trusted AutoService Since 1985

For over 30 years Auto Repair has been caring for cars and trucks in communities across the U.S. and Canada. The first in the industry to offer written estimates before work was performed and the first to give customers a view into service bays, Auto Repair has always stood by our pledge to do right by you and your car.

Our famous Auto Repair Lifetime Guarantee* on brakes, mufflers and exhaust, and shocks and struts means that we’ve got you covered, for as long as you own your car.

We don’t paint mufflers gold anymore, but our word is as good as gold. Count on your local Midas for all your car care needs. Trust the Auto Repair.
